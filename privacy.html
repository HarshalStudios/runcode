1. Introduction

RunCode ("we", "our", or "us") is an Android-based offline code editor and compiler designed for students, developers, and programming enthusiasts. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how we protect your privacy when you use the RunCode application.

2. Your Code and Files

Your source code, projects, folders, and programming files remain your property. RunCode does not upload, read, analyze, or store your code on our servers.

All files are managed locally using Android's Storage Access Framework (SAF), which means the application can only access files and folders that you explicitly choose.

  • Local Storage Only — Your projects remain on your device.
  • No File Scanning — We never scan your storage for unrelated files.
  • Your Ownership — You retain full ownership of every project you create.

3. Information We Collect

RunCode collects only the minimum information required to improve application quality and provide optional services.

  • Anonymous crash reports
  • Anonymous performance diagnostics
  • Application version
  • Android version
  • Device model

We never collect the contents of your source code, project files, or personal documents.

4. Third-Party Services

RunCode uses trusted Google services to provide core functionality.

  • Firebase Analytics
    Used to understand anonymous application usage and improve future updates.
  • Firebase Crashlytics
    Used to receive anonymous crash reports so bugs can be fixed more quickly.
  • Google AdMob
    Displays advertisements in the free version of RunCode. Google may process advertising identifiers according to its own Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing
    Handles purchases securely through Google Play. We never receive or store your payment information.

5. Permissions

RunCode requests only the permissions required for its intended functionality.

  • Internet
    Required for advertisements, analytics, crash reporting, and optional online features.
  • Storage Access Framework (SAF)
    Used only after you explicitly choose files or folders to open or save.

RunCode does not request unnecessary or invasive Android permissions.

6. Advertising

The free version of RunCode may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Advertising partners may collect limited device identifiers in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. RunCode itself does not sell or share your personal information with advertisers.

7. Data Security

Protecting your projects is one of our highest priorities.

  • Your files remain stored locally.
  • RunCode does not upload your projects.
  • The application follows Android security best practices.
  • Internal application data is protected using Android's secure storage model.

8. Children's Privacy

RunCode is intended for students, educators, and developers. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any future changes will be published on this page together with the updated revision date.
